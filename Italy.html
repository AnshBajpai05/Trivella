About Italy

Italy, officially known as the Italian Republic, is a country in Southern Europe famous for its art, culture, and history. It is home to the greatest number of UNESCO World Heritage Sites in the world, and its cuisine, fashion, and sports cars are renowned globally.

The Italian cities, such as Rome, Florence, and Venice, are known for their iconic landmarks, including the Colosseum, Uffizi Gallery, and St. Mark's Basilica. Explore Italy's rich history, visit ancient ruins, and indulge in authentic Italian food and wine.

Experience the breathtaking landscapes of Italy, from the stunning Amalfi Coast to the picturesque Tuscan countryside. Italy is also famous for its vibrant festivals, lively street markets, and world-class opera and music performances.

Things to Do

  • Visit the Colosseum in Rome
  • Explore the canals of Venice
  • Admire the art at the Uffizi Gallery in Florence
  • Relax on the Amalfi Coast
  • Indulge in pizza and pasta in Naples
